TTL Accuracy Evaluation

Accuracy reflects the share captured within the delay window. Remaining QPS is the delayed traffic that still falls outside that TTL.

Trade-off summary

60m already captures 98.00% TPAT and 99.50% HB notification traffic. 1d raises the capture rate to 99.20% and 99.92%, but the full-transaction KVDB footprint reaches about 91.8 TB on the HB transaction basis. 3d and 6d provide smaller incremental accuracy gains while multiplying storage and expiry compaction pressure.

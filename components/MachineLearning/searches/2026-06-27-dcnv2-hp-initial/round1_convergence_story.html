Capacity is not the blocker. Wide, small, cross=1, cross=3, cross=5, emb=16, and emb=32 land in the same validation band when training is healthy.

Round 2 Feature Exploration Targets

These are diagnostics first, then ablation candidates if the signal is real.
Price distribution Trailing p25, p50, p75, p90, std, and cv of highest bid price. Test whether high-error pockets have wider price spread.
Floor lifecycle Bid floor, mediation floor, overwritten floor, direct floor, and ratios to recent price quantiles.
Bid scarcity DSP bid count, no-bid rate, header-bidding hit rate, timeout, and top-bid spread.
Video/appopen context Ad size, flat CPM flags, publisher payout type, supply source, supply fee, and ad unit level aggregates.
Shallow tree probe Predict high residual using current features versus candidate features. A large lift gives fast evidence before DCNv2 retrain.
Ablation proof Final evidence still needs model-side addition and holdout validation. Distribution checks are the trigger, not the proof.
Suggested narrative: Round 1 did not fail. It completed the optimization search for the current signal set. The evidence says we are at a model/feature information boundary, so Round 2 should start by validating whether residual pockets contain missing price volatility, bid scarcity, or floor lifecycle signal.
